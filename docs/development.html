Achieve Your Goals

In this chapter, charlie learns from Micheal the wise teachings from Mr.Davis that success is not accidental. Micheal explains to Charlie that success is rather achieved through intentional planning. This intentional planning is in the form of having clear written goals, which gives direction and purpose to life. Without goals, persons tend to drift and settle for less. This chapter emphasizes the importance of setting specific, measurable, attainable and time bound goals as well as writing them down and reviewing them regularly.

Personal Alignment

This lesson connects beautifuly to both my academic and athletic life. As a track athlete, I have learned the importance of setting specific targets, like improving on my pbs and attaining certain qualification standards. In training I have also learned to set small, measurable goals that lead up to big achievements. This same principle applies to my studies, by setting academic goals such as maintaining a 3.7 GPA and completing my degree on time. Writing down my goals, whether athletic or academic, helps me visualize success and work toward it constantly.

The Proper Use of Time

This chapter highlights that everyone has the same twenty four hours every single day, but how it is used makes the difference. Successful people prioritze tasks that pushes them closer to their goals, while less successful people waste time on distractions. Effective time management means valuing each moment and investing it wisely.

Surround Yourself with the Best People

This chapter explains how the people that we associate with greatly influence our success. Our friends, mentors and teammates shape our mindset and habits. When we surround ourself with positive, motivated and hardworking people, they help us grow while negative influences hold us back.
